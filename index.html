About me


I'm Lincy Ellermeijer, 24 years old. I studied HBO-ICT:Game Development at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. Playing games has always been a hobby of mine, ever since I was a kid!

Nowadays, I work as a lecturer at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences, teaching courses to HBO-ICT students. During my bachelor's I have acquired knowledge and experience in different programming languages, methods and techniques. Today, I can teach these techniques to a new generation.

My main goal is to broaden my knowledge and develop my skills as both a programmer and a designer. I am studing at the University of Amsterdam for my Master's degree in Science. I want to expand my knowledge and experience, and develop myself further as an all-round Game Developer.
