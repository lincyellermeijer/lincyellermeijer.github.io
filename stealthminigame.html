Stealth Minigame

Information

This is a solo project I've worked on in my spare time to learn more about the lighting in Unity. It is a 3D environment with a player, some enemies and light effects. I also made the UI (menu's, pause menu, settings etc). The player is in a dark room and has a flashlight which he can turn on/off. He can walk around but when he runs into an enemy patrolling the area, the flashlight of the enemy turns red as an indicator that the player has been seen. The enemy will start "hunting" the player until he has been caught (gameover) or out of sight (enemy returns to their original position and resumes patrolling). When the player sees an enemy with their flashlight, this light will turn orange as a warning. I also added some light effects to lamps on the walls and more.

Overview
