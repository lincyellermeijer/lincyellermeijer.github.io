Treasure Hack

About

This is a big project I've worked on during my internship at Gamedia for Tweakers. I made the prototype for the third minigame. This is a top-down minigame where you have to proceed through the room while avoiding the cameras. You can change the order of the cameras by accesing the terminal and changing the code. The other main aspects I've worked on are the special effects. I've created all the particle effects in the game and added sound effects to them, like the rain at the start and the sparks in the first minigame. I also added some small animations (for example: the headlights of the car at tha start). Besides that I also worked on the animations of the comic (pages, text balloons etc.) with the plugin iTween. The game was available online on the website of Tweakers.

Overview
