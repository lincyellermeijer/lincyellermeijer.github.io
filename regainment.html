Regainment

About

Regainment is a puzzle, platformer game in which you control two characters in a mirror dimension. Regainment is about a character who has lost his memories and starts an epic journey to regain these. He has to travel through the mirror dimension, while overcoming difficult challenges. His end goal is to reach the God of Mirrors and ask to be reunited with part of his soul that was cast to the mirror dimension. Regainment is about overcoming challenges, exploring the mirror dimension and solving difficult puzzles. The player experience we are aiming for is a non-competitive, mind-driven gaming experience which can be played on your own speed. The game can be classified as an adventurous platformer with logic puzzles, which require a good set of brains. This game is designed for (young) adults who enjoy challenging puzzles which they can explore at their own pace in an immersive and enchanting environment. It is an ongoing project in a team of two.
