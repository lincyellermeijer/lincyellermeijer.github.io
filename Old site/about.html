About me:

Hiya!

I'm Lincy Ellermeijer, 20 years old. I study Game Development at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. Games were always one of my favorite things to do, ever since I was a kid!

Before I started this study I never programmed any games. I always loved to draw and be creative, so at first I strictly wanted to be a Game Designer. When I started programming, I came to the conclusion that this was more interesting than I'd ever imagined.

Now my main goal is to broaden my knowledge and develop my skills as both a programmer and a designer. I love working in a team, but also making games on my own and learning new things. I want to make more, better and a lot of different games!

If you have any questions or want to contact me, leave a message or check the contact page! Thank you for taking a look at my portfolio!

~ Lincy
